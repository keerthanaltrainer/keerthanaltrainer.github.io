About Keerthana L

I am Keerthana L, a Corporate Trainer and Behavioural Transformation Specialist with over 8 years of experience facilitating learning interventions across corporates, educational institutions, employability projects, and faculty development initiatives.

My expertise lies in creating highly interactive and experiential learning environments that encourage participants to engage, reflect, and apply learning in real-world situations.

I believe meaningful transformation occurs when individuals are given opportunities to realise their potential, reflect on their experiences, and implement actionable change.
